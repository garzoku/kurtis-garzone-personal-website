The changes I've made to this website

My girlfriend works for a cellular network provider and deals with technology on a daily basis. I asked her to sit down and navigate my website and let me know if she saw anything that needs improvement. Below are the changes I've made based on her analysis.

After

My About Me page originally had a black and white photo which aligned with the paragraph beside it. She suggested I use a color photo that reaches to the footer.

Before

After

I placed a favicon on the page because I wanted to try and include one. However, she told me it was too small to read. I removed it from the navigation bar.

After
